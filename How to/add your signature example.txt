Outlook Users:

 Click File > Options > Mail > Signatures.
Open your signature Website in the below table.

Press CTRL+A to Select All

Press CTRL+C to Copy All

Paste your signature into the box with CTRL+V.
When you're done, select Save > OK.

 
Office 365 Webmail Login (if you open a web browser for E-Mail):

 Go to Settings. > View all Outlook settings > Compose and reply.
Under the Email signature, you will see your Signature.

Open your signature Website in the below table.

Press CTRL+A to Select All

Press CTRL+C to Copy All

Paste your signature into the box with CTRL+V.

Select Save when you're done.
